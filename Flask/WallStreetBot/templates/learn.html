What is Postup

This is a light weight software application written and hosted by college student web developers and software engineers. It is intended to be used as a platform to boost exposure for a product or service.

Fully Automated

The days of Direct Messaging people to post on your behalf are over. Using APIs, we are able to automatically post to our Instagram, Facebook, or Twitter accounts when you submit an image and decription on our webpage.
